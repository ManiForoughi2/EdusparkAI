The Complete Process

Three simple steps to create comprehensive, engaging lesson kits

1

Enter Your Requirements

30 seconds

Simply describe what you want to teach and let our AI understand your needs.

  • Specify the topic or subject matter
  • Select grade level and student age range
  • Choose educational standards (Common Core, NGSS, etc.)
  • Add any specific learning objectives
  • Include preferred teaching methods or activities
2

AI Generates Complete Lesson Kit

2-3 minutes

Our advanced AI creates a comprehensive, standards-aligned lesson package tailored to your specifications.

  • Detailed lesson plan with clear objectives
  • Interactive PowerPoint presentation with VBA macros
  • Multiple assessment types (quizzes, tests, homework)
  • Student handouts and worksheets
  • Teacher guide with implementation tips
  • Differentiated materials for various learning levels
3

Export & Customize

1 minute

Download your complete lesson kit in multiple formats and customize as needed.

  • Export to DOCX and PDF formats
  • Download PowerPoint with embedded VBA macros
  • Generate Google Forms for assessments
  • Export QTI files for LMS integration
  • Customize content to match your teaching style
  • Share with colleagues or save to your library
